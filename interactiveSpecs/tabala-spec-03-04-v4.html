El meu perfil
Try it: tap a group card to open its detail. Scroll down through history → achievements → member list — all in one continuous screen.
Group card — list fields
Uniform colour panel
Left side. 3 horizontal bands: shirt (top+bottom, 2/5), sash (middle, 1/5), trousers (bottom, 2/5). Proportions fixed.
Group name
Bold, 1 line, truncated with ellipsis if too long.
Location
Pin icon + city. 1 line.
Stats row
3 columns: Fundada (year) · Membres (count) · Premis (count). Label small above, value bold below.
Star rating
5 stars + decimal score (e.g. 4.8). Shown below stats.
Filters
Horizontal scrollable chip row above list. Active chip = red. "Totes" selected by default.
Group detail — scroll order
1
Logo + name + subtitle
Circular logo · name · "Fundada YYYY · City"
2
Stats bar
Membres · Torres · Premis — separated by border
3
Group photo
Full-width rounded image, 90px tall
4
La nostra història
Free text. Scrolls if long
5
Assoliments
Coloured achievement cards. Newest first
6
Membres de la Muixeranga
Member rows with role badge + seniority. "Veure tots" button at bottom
Navigation
All sections in a single scrollable screen. No tabs. No pagination. Continuous scroll from top to bottom.
Back action
Back arrow in top bar returns to the group list.

Member list — row fields
Avatar
Profile photo or emoji placeholder. 36×36px circle.
Name + role title
Full name bold. Role/title below in grey (e.g. "Cap de muixeranga").
Position badge
Coloured pill right. Baix (pink) · Anxaneta (amber) · Segon (purple) · Terç (blue) · Dosos (green) · Pinya (orange).
Seniority
"N anys" below badge. Years in the group.
"Veure tots" button
Outline button at end of preview list. Opens full member screen.

Edge cases
Behaviour rules for boundary scenarios.
No search results
Empty state: "No s'ha trobat cap muixeranga amb «[text]»." Filter chips remain visible.
Unknown founding year
Fundada stat hidden entirely. No dash or placeholder shown.
Identical uniform colours
1px separator line at 20% black between same-colour bands to keep 3 zones readable.
No achievements yet
Section title shown; empty state inside: "No achievements recorded yet."
Member list on other group
The Membres section is hidden entirely — section title and all rows absent.
Very long history text
Text wraps and scrolls naturally within the single-screen detail. No "read more" truncation.
